start

to play, the group choose must first a chairman.

this is you.

the chairman deals each player 7 cards. the players may not touch the cards until the game begins.

the chairman chooses a player and a direction (clockwise or counter-clockwise). they then turn over the top card of the deck and announce the beginning of the game.


how to play

to win the game of mao, you need to get rid of all your cards.

to play a card, it must be higher than the last card played. ace is highest, 2 is lowest. once you hit ace, the order loops back around.

when a player places their last card to win, they must announce "mao."


the rules

all the rules are unspoken of. players must figure out the rules as they play.

if a player plays a card without following its accompanying rule, they receive a penalty. they must retrieve one card from the deck.


point of order

no one is allowed to speak during the game unless a rule requires it.

players are allowed to call "point of order" at any point.

everyone must put their cards down.

to end, the same player who called the point of order must announce "end point of order."
